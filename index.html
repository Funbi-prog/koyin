Brand Story

Koyin Sanusi is popularly known as “The Last Man Standing” the breakout finalist who emerged Second Runner-Up on Big Brother Naija Season 10. Since the show, he’s converted spotlight into real market value: campaigns, commercials, appearances and a brand that sells.

He’s worked with reputable names including Guinness Africa, BetKing, POCO Nigeria, Sonmade Luxury, and much more.. From high visibility launches to on camera commercials and premium brand partnerships, Koyin shows up as the modern face of culture polished, punctual, and camera ready.

Accented by awards recognition including AVA Awards: People’s Choice Award of the Year — Koyin is the “action guy” with a clean record of impact: distinctive presence, strong range, and work that reads premium on first glance.
