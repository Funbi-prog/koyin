My name is Samod Koyinsola Sanusi, but most people know me as Koyin. Born on August 19, 2003, I’m a model, actor, and recording artist.

By age 10 I was writing songs and dancing at every opportunity. In 2022 I found my lane in fashion and modeling—presence met passion and a new chapter began.

I’ve appeared in 50+ music videos for Nigeria’s biggest names Wizkid (IDK), Olamide (Kai), Rema (Benin Boys), and Flavour (Ayagaya), alongside brand collaborations with Coca-Cola, Flying Fish, Spotify, Verve, and UBA.

In 2024 I joined Big Brother Naija Season 10, finished Top 3, and built the community Koyinators × Kokomelon. Now I’m focused on global work across fashion, luxury, film, and beverage.
